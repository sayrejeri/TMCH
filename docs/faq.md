# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

This page answers common questions about **Total Miner Forge** and the **TotalMiner Community Hub (TMCH)**.  
If your question isn’t listed here, feel free to ask in the Discord.

---

## What is TotalMiner Community Hub (TMCH)?

TMCH is a **community-driven documentation site** for **Total Miner Forge**.  
Its goal is to collect, organize, and explain game systems that are often confusing, undocumented, or spread across different sources.

TMCH is **not officially affiliated** with the developers of Total Miner.

---

## Who maintains TMCH?

TMCH is maintained by members of the Total Miner community.  
Content is written, reviewed, and expanded as the community learns more about the game.

Anyone can contribute improvements or corrections.

---

## Is this documentation official?

No.  
TMCH is a **fan-made project**. While the information is carefully written and tested, it should be treated as community knowledge rather than official documentation.

---

## How accurate is the information?

Accuracy is a priority, but some mechanics are based on:
- in-game testing
- community discoveries
- observed behavior rather than formal documentation

If something is incorrect or outdated, it can be corrected through contributions.

---

## How can I contribute to the docs?

There are several ways to help:
- Fix typos or unclear explanations
- Expand existing pages
- Add new pages for undocumented systems
- Share discoveries from testing or experimentation

Use the **Edit this page** link on any page to suggest changes through GitHub.

---

## I found a mistake. What should I do?

You can:
- Submit a fix using **Edit this page**
- Open an issue on GitHub
- Mention it in the Discord so it can be corrected

Even small fixes help.

---

## Where should I ask questions?

For questions or discussion:
- Join the Discord: https://discord.gg/MWgeYF7Ymp
- Post in **[#help-desk](https://discord.com/channels/1453625103920267378/1453625109980909661)** for help
- Post in **[#suggestions](https://discord.com/channels/1453625103920267378/1453625110559985758)** for improvements or ideas

The **#docs** channel contains direct links to all documentation pages.

---

## Why doesn’t this page cover everything?

Some systems in Total Miner Forge are:
- poorly documented
- partially understood
- still being explored by the community

TMCH grows over time as more information becomes available.

---

## Can I use this documentation for my server or world?

Yes.  
You’re free to reference or link to TMCH for guides, explanations, or learning purposes.

If you reuse content, crediting the community is appreciated.

---

## Will TMCH stay up to date?

TMCH is updated when:
- mechanics change
- new discoveries are made
- contributors submit improvements

The **Last updated** date on each page shows when it was most recently revised.

---

## Is there a changelog?

Yes.  
Major updates and changes are tracked in the **Changelog** section of the documentation.

---

## Still have questions?

If you didn’t find what you were looking for:
- Ask in Discord
- Check the relevant system page
- Or suggest an FAQ addition

TMCH improves through community feedback.
